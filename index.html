The APPI Compliance Gap

Under APPI, data like device identifiers and IP addresses are "Personally Related Information" (PRI). Handling this data incorrectly creates significant legal risk.

Personally Related Information (PRI) Includes:

  • 💻 Device & Hardware IDs
  • 🌐 IP Address
  • 📱 Advertising Identifiers (IDFA/AAID)
  • 💽 Application Integrity Markers
